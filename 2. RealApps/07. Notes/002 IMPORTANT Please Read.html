About Videos Quality

Udemy uses Dynamic Adaptive Streaming for video delivery. That means that it tries to deliver videos at the highest usable quality for each specific user.

From the experience of many students sometimes it fails and you'll see blurred videos. If you know you have a decent Internet connection, the best you can do is to try to set manually the resolution to 720p in Udemy web player (by default it's set to Auto).

This is NOT a course problem (each lecture is FullHD or HD quality), but an Internet slow connection or a Udemy technical issue. If the problem persists, please get in touch with me to see how we can solve it.
